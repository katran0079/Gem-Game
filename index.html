Miner Problem

Story / How to Play

There are legends of a cave filled with precious, priceless gems hidden deep within the rain forest. You are one of the lucky few explorers to have navigated your way to its entrance. Your goal is to mine and collect 210 units of gems. Click the gems to mine/collect them! Each gem is worth a different amount of units and their worth changes with every trip.
